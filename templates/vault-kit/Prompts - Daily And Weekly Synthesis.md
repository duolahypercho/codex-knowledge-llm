# Prompts - Daily And Weekly Synthesis

Created by: {{OWNER}}

## Daily Brief

```text
Read everything in /inbox from the last 24 hours and everything in /notes from the last 7 days.

Return:
CONNECTIONS - 3 interesting links between recent captures and older notes.
PATTERN - 1 pattern across this week's notes.
QUESTION - 1 question worth sitting with today.

Write a clean Obsidian markdown brief.
```

## Weekly Synthesis

```text
Read the vault. Focus on everything added in the last 7 days.

Return:
EMERGING THESIS - What idea is forming?
CONTRADICTIONS - What conflicts across my notes?
KNOWLEDGE GAPS - What perspective is missing?
ONE ACTION - What is the highest-leverage action this week?

Be direct. Challenge me. Do not summarize what I already know.
```

## Session Summary

```text
Create inbox/session-summary-YYYY-MM-DD.md.

Include what changed, decisions made, open questions, next actions, and related notes.
```
